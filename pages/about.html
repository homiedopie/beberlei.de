About

My name is Benjamin Eberlei and I have been writing this blog on and off since 2005. Not all posts survived migrating through different blog engines since then, only those starting in 2008 and older. I am a programmer, trainer and consultant and since recently also a founder of the company Tideways, where we trace, profile and monitor PHP applications.

I have blogged about a wide range of topics including programming, software architecture and craftsmanship, Symfony, Doctrine, Ansible, Zend Framework 1, Vagrant, Side Projects. If you are interested in some of the historic discussions please have a look at the archives.

If you don’t want to miss any new content, then you should follow me on Twitter or subscribe to the RSS feed of this blog.
